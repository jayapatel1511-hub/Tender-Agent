HRCE-4299 Dartmouth High School Recladding and Window Replacement

This fixture is a local proposal-agent extract for V1 testing. It is not a substitute for the live Nova Scotia Procurement Portal package.

Submission instructions:
Proponents must submit through the Nova Scotia Procurement Portal before the stated closing date and time. Late submissions will not be accepted.

Mandatory submission forms:
The proposal must include a completed submission form, signed addenda acknowledgement, conflict of interest declaration, safety plan, project schedule, and pricing form.

Addenda:
Proponents are responsible for reviewing all addenda issued before closing and incorporating addenda into the final proposal.

Pricing:
The pricing form must identify all labour, materials, equipment, allowances, and taxes. Pricing must remain valid for 60 days after closing.

Quality and safety:
The proposal shall describe quality control, site safety, school access coordination, and protection of existing building occupants.

Rated criteria:
The evaluation will consider relevant project experience, methodology, schedule, safety approach, quality management, and price.
