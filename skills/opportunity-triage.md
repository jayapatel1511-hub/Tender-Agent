# Opportunity Triage

Purpose: classify new tender matches for pursuit relevance.

## Prefer

- engineering services
- design services
- feasibility studies
- assessments
- capacity or assimilative capacity studies
- stormwater, wastewater, water, municipal infrastructure
- planning, modelling, inspection, condition assessment

## Separate Or Skip

- construction-heavy tenders
- supply, equipment, vendor, or goods-only procurements
- installation-only work
- items with no professional-services scope

## Categories

- `prime-consultant-fit`
- `partner-or-subconsultant-fit`
- `likely-skip`
- `needs-review`

## Output

For each relevant tender, include title, buyer, closing date, source URL, relevance reason, and recommended next action.
